02 — Working principles

A good system does more than work on a normal day.

  1. 01

    Design for failure. Treat degradation, retries, and idempotency as part of the primary flow.

  2. 02

    Measure before optimizing. Start with latency, saturation, and operational signals instead of intuition.

  3. 03

    Roll out with a way back. Use observability, feature flags, and canaries to reduce blast radius.
